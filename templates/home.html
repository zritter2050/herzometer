Input Variables

enter age in years


sex, 0 = female, 1 = male

resting blood pressure (mm Hg on admission to the hospital)


cholesterol in mg/dl


fasting blood sugar (> 120 mg/dl, 1 = true, 0 = false)


resting electrocardiographic measurement (0 = normal, 1 = having ST-T wave abnormality, 2 = probable or definite left ventricular hypertrophy)


maximum heart rate achieved


exercise induced angina (1 = yes; 0 = no)


ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest (ST relates to positions on the ECG plot)


slope of the peak exercise ST segment (0 = upsloping, 1 = flat, 2 = downsloping


number of major vessels (0-3)


on thalassemia blood disorder (1 = normal, 2 = fixed defect, 3 = reversable defect)
